# WASP-39b Transit Analysis

**Date:** 2026-03-15 17:49:44
**Tags:** exoplanet, WASP-39b, transit, JWST, atmosphere

---

WASP-39b is a hot Saturn orbiting WASP-39 with a period of 4.055 days.
Transit depth computed at ~2.03%, easily detectable from ground.
The planet is well outside the habitable zone (T_eq = 1166 K).
JWST has detected H2O, CO2, SO2, Na, and K in its atmosphere.
This makes it one of the best-characterized exoplanet atmospheres.
